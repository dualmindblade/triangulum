Wanted simulation dynamics:

Initialization
A spherical planet as earthlike as possible

Step 1
Generate plate geometry and direction/motion with subdivided subplates and historical plate boundaries
Use this information with noise to generate a rough map of the terrain.


Step 2
Weather (Atmosphere and ocean currents)
Some kind of simulation of these dynamics over the terrain map
Collect seasonal information: rainfall, temperature, atmospheric currents, ocean currents, cloud cover, etc.


Step 3
Hydraulics (rivers, erosion, deposition)
Using the precipitation data from above, simulate erosion and river/lake formation and sediment deposition

Step 4
Refine weather simulation using the new elevation data (possibly just re-run the original simulation)

Step 4
Biome formation
Use information collected to calculate Koppen climate types over the land
